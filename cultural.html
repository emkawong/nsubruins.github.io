ABOUT

History

Since the 1986 Week of Remembrance, the Nikkei Student Union’s Annual Cultural Night has served to commemorate President Roosevelt’s signing of Executive Order 9066—which led to the incarceration of 110,000 Japanese Americans across the West Coast into internment camps—as well as to spread Japanese American culture to the UCLA community and beyond.

The theme of the show changes each year to address current issues and topics in the Los Angeles Japanese American community, presenting a unique experience with each and every show. Our 31st Annual Cultural Night, Chirashi, will reflect on the different motivations that lead people to be involved in their respective communities. It will also include amazing performances by NSU Modern, NSU Odori, and Kyodo Taiko!
